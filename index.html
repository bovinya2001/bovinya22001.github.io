Our Hotels

At each of our hotel branches, we have meticulously crafted spaces where comfort and relaxation are the forefront of your stay.
From the moment you step through our doors, you'll be greeted by a warm ambiance that instantly sets the tone for a memorable visit.

Tamraparni Kandy

Nestled in Kandy's lush hills, Thamraparni offers an elegant retreat, blending nature's grandeur with luxury.With tranquil gardens, opulent interiors, and warm service, it's a haven for those seeking serenity and sophistication. Discover an enchanting escape at Thamraparni.

Tamraparni Galle

Nestled in historic Galle, Thamraparni seamlessly blends heritage and modern luxury.Surrounded by the city's charm and ocean vistas, it's a haven of tranquility.With tasteful rooms, delectable cuisine, and attentive service,Thamraparni Galle offers a refined coastal retreat.

Tamraparni Arugam bay

Thamraparni Arugam Bay is a coastal oasis of timeless elegance, where pristine beaches meet luxury.From refined accommodations to attentive service,it's an exquisite escape for discerning travelers
